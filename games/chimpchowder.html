Chimp Chowder is an upcoming game announced on May 20, 2022 in which the player sits amongst two chimpanzees that require constant spoon-fulls of chowder to remain calm, eventually killing the player if their demands aren't met quickly enough.

The game is currently on the back burner but will be released on Steam as early access upon completion, with plans of an eventual mobile port.
