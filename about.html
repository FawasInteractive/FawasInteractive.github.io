ABOUT US

Fawas Interactive

Fawas Interactive first made its entry into the game industry to fulfill the creative itch of an avid gamer, with the goal of creating various amounts of fun and unique experiences.

Beginning in 2017 (under the former name: FawaStudios), a large amount of nearly lost-to-time projects were created, some bad, and some downright horrible. However, amidst that steaming pile of garbage came some very valuable lessons and a lot of experience.

Flashing forward, Fawas Interactive is still moving along in pursuit of its original goal, with no plans to stop. We sincerely hope you join us as we continue to learn, grow, and create.

Meet the Team (of One)

As for me, my name's Ethan, and I'm the creative force behind Fawas Interactive. I'm a picky game enthusiast from Ohio, USA with a love of most things programming, messing with other creative skills such as: marketing, art, and pretty much everything required for solo development.

I'm very passionate about what I do, and Fawas Interactive gives me an outlet to bring out and share my love for things like Halloween, first-person shooters, camping, and all things spooky.

When I'm not incessantly rambling to peers about my game dev endeavors, I can be found coming up with overcomplicated lore in Minecraft, playing my thousandth hour in Left 4 Dead 2, or grinding my crowbar skills in Project Zomboid.
